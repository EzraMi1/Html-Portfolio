Top Three Movies of all Time

The Dark Knight

Excellent storyline, coupled with incredible acting and scenes that make it impossible to look away.

Whiplash

Whiplash is a film about a drummer striving to be the greatest in the world! On his journey to the top he encounters a physically and emotionally abusive teacher who has his best interests at heart. Does he take things too far or will the drummers dream allow him to endure anything?

Marvel Avengers:Endgame

Great action with incredibly choreographed fight scenes and toe curling moments of suspense!
